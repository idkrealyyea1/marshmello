Our Story

A Legacy of Timeless
Elegance

Founded in 1998, Grand Élysée has been the cornerstone of luxury celebrations in the region. Nestled across 12 breathtaking acres, our estate combines classical architecture with contemporary sophistication to create a setting that is nothing short of extraordinary.

From intimate wedding ceremonies to grand corporate galas, our dedicated team of 200+ professionals orchestrates each event with meticulous care, ensuring that every detail transcends expectation.

Frequently Asked
Questions

We believe in complete transparency. Find the answers to our most common questions below, or contact our concierge team for personalized assistance.

Contact Concierge

We recommend booking at least 12 to 18 months in advance for peak-season events such as weddings and large galas, especially for Saturdays from May through October. Corporate events and weekday bookings are often available with shorter notice. Contact our concierge to check real-time availability.

Our Grand Ballroom can accommodate up to 800 guests for a seated dinner or 1,200 guests for a cocktail reception. The Garden Terrace comfortably hosts up to 400 guests, while our Royal Suite is perfect for intimate gatherings of up to 80 guests. We offer multiple configurations to suit your vision.

Yes. We offer fully bespoke all-inclusive packages covering venue, catering, floral design, lighting, entertainment coordination, photography and videography partnerships, bridal suites, valet parking, and dedicated concierge service. Every package is tailored to your specific requirements and budget during an initial consultation.

We understand every couple and client has unique preferences. While we take great pride in our in-house Michelin-star culinary team and curated vendor network, we are delighted to work with your preferred vendors. All external vendors must be pre-approved and comply with our operational standards and insurance requirements.

A 30% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your date. Full payment is due 30 days prior to the event. Our cancellation policy offers a partial refund (50% of remaining balance) if cancelled more than 90 days before the event date. We strongly recommend event insurance, which we can help arrange on your behalf.

We offer complimentary valet parking for up to 500 vehicles on our estate grounds. Additional overflow parking is available for larger events through our partner facilities nearby. VIP and handicap-accessible spaces are always reserved closest to the main entrance.
